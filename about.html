About

Learn more about my experiences and professional skills.

A freelance developer and designer

My name is Lucas Mauess, I am a independent web developer and designer from Brazil. I work with planning, design, development and optimization of websites, web/mobile applications and UI/UX projects for digital products. I am currently working as a freelancer for small and medium businesses, personal customers and as partner or consultant to digital agencies and startups.

I specialize in front-end development, with focus on responsive design, performance, interactivity, cross-device compatibility, accessibility and optimization strategies. Always with an eye for innovation, I combine my knowledge, skills and experience to deliver the best project possible. I am a person very communicative and love the possibility of working together with creative people and awesome teams.

I live in Belém, on middle of the Amazon (the largest rainforest of the world). But here is not a jungle, is the capital of the state of Pará and largest city in the country's north. Here I study to undergraduate in Computer Science at Federal University of Pará (UFPA). And the same way I love living here, I like to travel and discover new challenging experiences, especially in the World Wide Web :)
